- **Dependency ratios** (youth dependency: ages 0–14; old-age dependency: 65+)
- **Working-age population** (typically 15–64, or 15–74 in some models)
- **Median age** of the population
- **Population pyramids** (age-sex distributions)
- **Cohort sizes** (e.g., Gen Z, millennials, baby boomers)
- **Old-age support ratios** (number of working-age people per elderly person)
