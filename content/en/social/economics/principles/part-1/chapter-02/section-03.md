## Replacing the Time Value of Money with the Time Value of Life

Part of the upgrade of human mentality to rise above crude matter into the subtle spirit or consciousness is the replacement of money with actual real exchangeable value in goods and services. 

This changes the focus of the mind from static money into dynamic creativity that each person puts into his work. 

This will be more evident if that person's work represents his dharma or inherent purpose in life. 

This is why we replace the store of value from being static, based on Time Value of Money, into something dynamic, based on the Time Value of Life.
