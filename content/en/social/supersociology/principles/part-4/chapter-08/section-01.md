Thinker Philosophies are created by or favor the thinker class, also known as the intellectuals or the brahmins in Hinduism. 

In the Republc by Plato, the thinker class is called the Aristocrats, as philosophers.

These are people who live by ideas, theories, or knowledge.

Thinkers thrive after their society has been made peaceful by warriors. This allows the peace that is needed by thinkers to think. 


The main feeling of thinkers is the need to think or to share ideas and beliefs. 

This desire to share beliefs often leads to religion or education or the spread of certain philosophies.
