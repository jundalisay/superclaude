Trader Philosophies are created by or favor the trader class, also known as the merchants, or the vaesyas in Hinduism. 

In the Republc by Plato, the worker class is called the Oligarchs.

These are people who live by trade, gaining riches with every deal. Unlike the workers who are happy with simple pleasures, the trader class has the most expensive ones. 

The trader class emerges towards the last phase of a society's cycle after the warrior class has kept the peace and the thinker class has sorted out the remaining problems. 

The trader class goes out to extend the reach, wealth, and influence of the society.
