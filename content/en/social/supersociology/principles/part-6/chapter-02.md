One of the main goals of Supersociology is the creation of a world government, which we will call an Earth Government (EG).

This is to differentiate it from other governments that:
- humans will set up in other planets or moons
- humans will discover in other planets

This can only be realized if the moral feelings of humans are unified into a common moral interest to create an Earth Government.

This will likely arise as a consequence of humans experiencing the horrors of war, such as how the European Union was created as a solution to World War 2. 

World War 3, during the crisis years that our model predicted to start in 2020, will be the likely cause of a push for an Earth Government. 

We propose to start it through an economic system called Multilateral Clearing, and through the advocacy of Constitutional Virtues.
