## The Planetary Health Diet

The **Planetary Health Diet**—rooted in mostly whole plant foods, moderate whole grains and healthy fats, and far less red meat and processed food—was designed to protect both people and the planet. 

It is also more spiritual as plants are known to have a higher vibration than animals, and is the reason why Asian philosophies push for plant-based diets. 

Beyond spirit, it delivers powerful benefits for everyone:
- It prevents nearly 20% of global early deaths by lowering risks of heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.
- It cuts greenhouse gas emissions by up to 70%, protects forests, and preserves precious water and land.
- It is kinder to all living beings, fostering compassion and respect for life in every form.

Eating this way is not just a choice for better health or a cooler planet—it is a way to live more consciously, more connected, and more in tune with the world we share.
