- **Part 8: The National Tax Code**
  - **Chapter 29: Income Taxation**
    - Definition of Residency: Resident Citizens, Aliens, and Non-Residents
    - Gross Income Inclusions and Exclusions (De Minimis Benefits, 13th Month Pay)
    - Allowable Deductions: Itemized Deductions vs. Optional Standard Deduction
    - Filing, Assessment, and Tax Refund Procedures
  - **Chapter 30: Transfer and Consumption Taxes**
    - Estate Tax: Gross Estate, Deductions (Funeral, Medical, Standard), and the Vanishing Deduction
    - Donor’s Tax: Rates, Exemptions (Dowries), and Political Contributions
    - Value-Added Tax (VAT): Thresholds, Zero-Rated vs. Exempt Transactions, Input Tax Credits
    - Excise Taxes on Sin Products (Alcohol, Tobacco, Sweetened Beverages), Petroleum, and Automobiles
  - **Chapter 31: Tax Remedies and Compliance**
    - Civil Penalties, Surcharges, and Interest
    - Criminal Offenses: Tax Evasion, Failure to Withhold, and False Returns
    - The Court of Tax Appeals Appellate Procedure
