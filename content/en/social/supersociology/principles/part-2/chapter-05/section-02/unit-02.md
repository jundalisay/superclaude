In Supersociology, the Constitution defines the oversoul of a society.

- The National Motto and Seal

- Supremacy Clause: The Constitution as the Supreme Law of the Land

### 2. National Territory and Sovereignty

- Definition of Terrestrial, Fluvial, Maritime, and Aerial Domains

- The State as a Democratic and Republican Entity

- Right of the People to Information and Transparency Mandate

- Due Process and Equal Protection
- Freedom of Speech, Press, Assembly, and Redress of Grievances
- Protection Against Unreasonable Searches and Seizures
