I began Superphysics in 2012 as a response to the 2008 Financial Crisis. I formed a team with people who were interested and realized there were 3 milestones for this lifetime:

1. The 1st milestone was the creation and set up for Superphysics. This is research and content creation. This is the abstract parts.

2. The 2nd milestone is the actual deployment of Superphysics in the real world. This includes technologies and experiments. This is the concrete parts.

3. The 3rd milestone will be the maintenance of the abstract and concrete parts through people that will continue the work. 

You could say that:
- the 1st milestone re-created the wheel of dharma
- the 2nd milestone made the wheel turn
- the 3rd milestone will keep the wheel turning


## The Last Milestone

We can the last milestone as the "Anu Het" goal.
