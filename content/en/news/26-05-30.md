We updated the MSQ model to add force as media-quanta (forces), as opposed to substance-quanta (particles). This is to balance the 3 types of neutrinos with the 3 types of 'weak force bosons'.


We hesitated to do this because this meant we had to also expand the MSQ for Bio Superphysics, which we are totally deficient in, as none of us work in the Western medical industry which has many things defined. So our defintions should match not only Western medicine but also Alternative medicine which we have some knowledge on. 

![MSQ](/graphics/bio/msqbio.svg)

As you can see, even without the force column, our MSQ for Bio Superphysics has a lot of blanks. So adding a force column just adds more blanks.
