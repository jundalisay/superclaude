Last year, we formalized the concept of the four properties of waves as:


1. Commonality (Relationality)
2. Density
3. Balance (Unit)
4. Spread


We first realized this in Supersociology and Supereconomics which had the 4 Social Cycles and 4 Laws of Value.

This led to us reorganizing the rest of the sub sciences of Superphysics such as:

- Spiritual Superphysics to be based on 4 paths
- Supermath to be based on 4 Operations of Add, Divide, Subtract, Multiply


This bloats Supermath and so we have to separte it from Superphysics. 

Since Supermath is just numerical logic or flow, then it means Superlogic, as Socratic Dialectics, will also be separated from Superphysics in the future.
