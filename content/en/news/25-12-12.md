Today we created a branch called Superhistory which lists the historical events for societies, countries, or civilizations. 

These lists are the basis for the social cycles as its training data. 

I began making the first lists in 2012 to predict the outcome of the Greek Debt Crisis. 

The list lets us trace cause and effect much bettter.

For example, the data from the [1177 BC Collapse of Civilizations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Bronze_Age_collapse) led us to [attribute it to the Anunnaki](/social/history/1177).
