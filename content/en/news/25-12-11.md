As Superphysics increased in scope and complexity, we began to use AI videos from the photos of our team members to explain the concepts to give a human connection. 

However, this needed consent of those members each time their images were used for animations. This created a bottleneck. 

So we changed to making pure AI videos. 

We began by making an AI Japanese woman named Sora, from the old name of our SORA or SOcial Resource Allocation.
