SATYA.Utilization of one's Want for the Hitam of others.
Want=Indriya(sensory and motor organs) and mind
Kalyam or Kshema:Spiritual welfare.
Shubam:Mental and Spiritual welfare.
Hitam:Physical,mental and spiritual welfare.
In satya, the mind and the indriyas shall express for hitam.
This is a very important upayantra for all others.
It is essential,because we need a balance mind to work.
Asha or hope is a very important vrti located in anahata cakra which guides each action,and for the materializaion of Asha,Satya is a must.
To express a particular event as it ocurrs,is Rta.

Conscious mind is guided by samskaras,but the subconscious can be iluminated,and it has a link with the unconscious.Then the clouds can be vanished and see the light of the causal mind.
Then we become allknowers.
