


VIDEHII SAM'SKA'RA (UNEMBODIED SAM'SKA'RA)

Spoken by Ba'ba' at Ranchi Jagrti on 16th May 1969 - When the A'tma' and mind are detached from the body, we call it physical death. The moment mind is dissociated from the body, Indriyas also stop functioning and without the help of the active Indriyas, mind cannot express its inherent momentum, it remains only in potential form, in the form of Sam'ska'ras. With the aid of Mutative Prakrti, they begin to move about here and there till they become well-adjusted with a suitable physical body. However the Videhii Ma'nasa (Unembodied mind) remains connected with Unit Consciousness but in absence of its association with Indriyas it cannot give expression to its inherent momenta. It requires the help of a body having connection with Indriyas. The vibrational waves of those Sam'ska'ras can be seen by advanced Sa'dhakas. The nature of Sam'ska'ras depends on the nature of one's actions performed, one who has done only virtuous deeds in life will be reborn an ideal man, learn and practise spiritual Sa'dhana' with exceptional progress, while those who will only eat, drink and be merry, are bound to be reborn as lower creatures. The Videhii Ma'nasa feels, but in absence of proper media of projection, they cannot express their inner feelings. A'tma' at this stage also remains as the witness to that unembodied mind. But it witnesses only this thing that his mind is not doing anything. It is a witnessing entity of the inactive mind. It sees and gains many experiences without any expression. But if Parama Purus'a gives some power of expression, they can express very little of their feeling. 

VIDEHII MANA (UNEMBODIED MIND)

Spoken by Ba'ba' on 17 May 1969 at Ranchi Jagrti: In a living body, A'tma' functions as a witness to whatever the mind does with the help of brain and Indriyas. When the mind thinks in terms of stealing and accordingly hands actually steal something, A'tma' immediately comes to know of it. This inalienable connection between mind and A'tma' is likened to that between a stage and the stage-lamp. Whatever dramatic performance was there on the stage is known to the lamp. Even when there is no performance on the stage, that also, is known to the lamp. But at the time of death, the mind being dissociated from the brain, moves, in association with A'tma', in the form of reactive momenta. It moves on till it gets a suitable structure by the grace of Mutative Prakrti. Maybe, it may have to wait a good many years or even ten lakhs of years to get a proper structure. Mind requires a physical body. It is the mind that controls the nerve-cells and nerve-fibres. But with death, one loses the same control over the nerve-cells and nerve-fibres. When the mind and A'tma' are detached from the body but life is suspended i.e. life can again come back, in that case, a very subtle link is left between the body and A'tma' & Mind, just as there is a subtle link between the flying kite in the sky and the wheel of thread in the boy's hand. Mind in association with A'tma' is just like a kite. But in case of actual death, this link is also cut off. At that time, the A'tma' which remains associated with the roaming Videhii Mana is called Su'tra'tma' and Parama'tma' who witnesses the Su'tra'tma' is known as Sutreshvara. 

SECRET OF BLESSING

(Spoken by Ba'ba' on 30th May, 1969 at Ranchi Ja'grti): The mental and spiritual progress of a Sa'dhaka depends upon the degrees of control, he possesses over the Vrttis. These Vrttis are fifty in number. Each Vrtti is controlled by a particular gland. The glands function mechanically. If the faces of glands are closed, secretion stops forthwith. Similarly, if the glandular secretion is doubled, the expression of the Vrttis also will be more. Thus all the psychic Vrttis such as fear, shyness etc. can be regulated. In this way, all the Vrttis can well be regulated. On the right and left of Manipura Cakra - the Vrttis of shyness and fear are located respectively. The controlling gland of Ka's'a'ya Vrtti (irritation) on the upper left of the Man'ipura Cakra. That is why, a patient of dyspepsia suffers from short temper. Now to bless somebody means to remove the defects in the physical glands that stand in the way of spiritual progress of a particular Sa'dhaka. When a disciple bows down to a Guru, Guru must bless him. By blessing, Guru helps in removing the defects of the glands. This should not be done in all cases. Hence, it is not desirable to accept Pran'a'mas of everybody. 

HORMONE SECRETION 

(Spoken by Ba'ba' on 29th May, 1969 at Ranchi Jagrti) A few glands, when joined together, form a plexus. There are seven such plexii in the human body. Different glands control different mental Vrttis through secretion of hormones. If the hormone-secretion of glands becomes defective, the mental expression is also defective. Now the balanced state of the mind depends upon the proper functioning of these glands, i.e. requisite hormone-secretion. As a rule, the higher Cakra controls the lower Cakra or Cakras. Thus the Sva'dhis't'ha'na Cakra controls the Mu'la'dha'ra, the Man'ipura controls the Sva'dhis't'ha'na and Mu'la'dha'ra, the Ana'hata controls the Man'ipura, Sva'dhis't'ha'na and Mu'la'dha'ra, the Vishuddha controls the four lower Cakras and the A'jina' controls the lower five Cakras and finally the Pineal Gland (Sahasra'ra Cakra) controls all the Cakras. When the Pineal secretion comes to A'jina' Cakra, from there it trickles still further down or the same may be burnt up due to one's impure mental thoughts. If the mind, at that time, remains full of divine thoughts, the secretion goes down and invigorates the lower Cakras which start secreting hormones in greater quantity than before. In addition to general secretion in a month, an extra amount of 1 or 1 1/2 drops of hormone is produced. If the Sa'dhaka's mind remains pure due to constant Cosmic Ideation, the extra amount is absorbed in his body and all the nerve-cells and nerve-fibres assimilate that hormone. He enjoys divine bliss. This is called God-intoxication. 

PRAN'AVA DHVANI

Ba'ba' was sitting as usual very graciously on the cot and started with these words, "This is the Sa'dhana' Year and the next year will be the Seminar Year". Again He said, "Of course, Sa'dhana' Year will be continued for an infinite period". He then ordered to close the door and windows so the external sounds may not disturb in any way. According to His command all the Sa'dhakas sat in Dhya'na'sana. Those who were imparted the lesson of Dhya'na were told to sit in Siddha'sana and those who [knew] were not, were told to sit in Padma'sana. All were then told to fix up their minds at the prescribed "Is't'a Cakra" but not to start Sa'dhana'. After sometime all excepting a few started to hear the sound as produced by the crickets. After sometime He told us to open our eyes and take a look at Him and again concentrate at the "Trikut'i" (for all persons) and to enjoy the Pran'ava Dhavani (which He told was the first of its kind to be experienced by Sa'dhakas). The sound now was more intensified and changed slowly into "Nu'pura-Dhvani". He repeated many times - "Enjoy this sound - this is the Divine Sound - the Pran'ava Dhvani". In the third stage He told all of us to take the mind concentrate at the Vishuddha - again He said, "Open your eyes and look at me for a second or two and then again concentrate". The Sa'dhakas then felt that the sound had become a bit feeble and slightly changed. He said, "It is now more melodious (Madhura) than what you heard at the two other points." At the end of this He said, "All of you have experienced the sound excepting a few only, those who have not heard should do more intensive Sa'dhana' for a few days and by their normal Sa'dhana', they will also hear this sound". He assured, "Those who I give this sound experience once will be able to listen this sound even while walking on the road only by remembering Me for a few seconds. I will complete the course of one and all who will only follow the Supreme Command." He finally explained that this was the "Sound of Silence" - the "Divine Sound" or the "Pran'ava Dhvani". "This sound is produced by the process of thinking of the Creator when He thinks for creation". The entire creation is within His mind and every action produces a particular sound, the average of all the sounds produced within is this sound - the sound caused by creation of a thing, sound due to existence of a thing and sound due to the destruction of a thing; as everything is happening only within Him. Everything is having its own individual vibration and every individual possesses different vibrations for different points and one average vibration at the nucleus of the entire body. He then told a boy (Sa'dhaka) to sit in Dhya'na'sana as per his choice with the spinal cord in proper position. He also told him to keep his navel (Na'bhi) exposed. He then touched with His stick the navel point of the boy. Some movement in the body was marked. His Ana'hata was then touched, the boy started crying aloud. He explained, "Ana'hata is the seat of all sentiments, so by a touch to it, all the sentiment are touched, so this cry occurred in him". His (the boy's) Vishuddha Cakra was touched - the boy was completely silent now (as his Vishuddha Cakra was touched). Finally He touched the Trikut'i of the boy and said, "The average individual vibration of the boy is now merged into the Cosmic Vibration, so he will enjoy the constant flow of Bliss." The boy fell flat on the ground. He commanded, "Enjoy now the Supreme Bliss - Enjoy". He pointed out that one should sit in Sa'dhana' always in proper position. (Ranchi Jagrti, Evening of 14th., Dec. '69) 

JYOTI, VIBHU'TI AND ANTARAYA'MITVA: 

In the evening Darshana at Ranchi Jagrti on 28th. January, 1970, there were 15 persons present. It started as the prelude to joint discussion. When we talk, our vital energy is converted into sound. In Nirvikalpa Sama'dhi, the vital energy is transformed into spiritual force. When the vital energy is transformed into spiritual energy, sound (Na'da) and effulgence (Jyoti) can be created. One can see the effulgence, if one is a bit spiritually developed. Nothing is a miracle in this universe. As to a primitive man, solar and lunar eclipses, tides and lightnings were miracles, but through gradual education when primitive people learnt the secrecy, it was no more a miracle to them. Similarly, when man by dint of his spiritual Sa'dhana' attains progress, there remains no miracles.

EGO - THE GREATEST ENEMY 

(9/2/1970 at Ranchi Jagrti): Darpa (ego) whether for good work or bad, it is harmful. A story goes like this, "Na'ra'yan'a as Baman Avata'ra asked for three steps of Bhu'mi from the King Bali as the king had the Darpa (ego) of his Da'na (charity). Bali at once agreed to give the desired land, since he thought the three steps of a dwarf man would be very small. Now the Baman Avata'ra put down his first step and the king started seeing his feet everywhere in the mundane world, now the Avata'ra put his second step in the A'ka'sha and the king started seeing his feet everywhere in the A'ka'sha. Now the Avata'ra asked the king, 'Where should I put my third step? The king replied - on my head, and when the Avata'ra put his third step on the head of the king, he immediately entered into the Pa'ta'la. Only one Aham'ka'ra of man can remain that he is the son of the Parama Purus'a. He can snatch away your object of Garva (ego) at any moment. Man is a Mana-Pradha'na creature and Pashu is Shariira- Pradha'na, therefore man has to be careful in his mental thinking. Mind is to be kept always pure. Gaunga' water and cow-dung is not required for this purpose.
