Relax and let people come into your life naturally while being real and true. 


## Being in Love

When you are in love, you will know what your beloved is feeling. When you have the same vibration, you have the same feelings and thoughts at the same time.

In love, all things are accomplished effortlessly. 



## Impatient for Love

A woman was asking why she still hasn't found a romantic partner as it is her passion to find love.

Bashar asked if she trusts the timing.

She hesitated as she was impatient. 

Bashar said that she was not meeting her lover because she was not yet the person she preferred to be.

And so she is still not attracting her lover. 

Bashar asks: Would she rather meet a man in her current unpreferred state?

She says no. 




## Be Complete (Elan)

To have a soulmate, you should be complete yourself first. This will give off a reflection that you already are complete. 

Do not think that you are incomplete and you need someone to complete you. 

Do not need each other to make each other complete. 

Realize that you are already complete. If not, then simply choose your preferred completeness. Then act that way!
