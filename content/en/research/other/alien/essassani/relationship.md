## Continue the relationship if it is what you Prefer. End if it is not. 

Relationships are for each person to reflect to each other who they are, so that they should grow.

If the vibrations are incompatible, they should let each other go so you can atract other people who need them.

Keep the relationship if there is growth to come from it. 

**But communication is key. **

The couple should have clear communication on what relationship is for.
