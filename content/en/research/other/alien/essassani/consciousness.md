1. Oversoul (Non-Physical / 7th Density / highest vibration )

This is a collection of many Individual Souls; a multi-dimensional, simultaneous awareness.

2. Individual Soul / Spirit (Non-Physical / 6th Density): The "afterlife" realm, which projects into many physical incarnations.

3. Higher Mind (Quasi-Physical / 5th Density)

This navigates time (past/present/future) as the physical mind navigates space. 

A parallel reality to the physical.

4. Template Level of Reality (Quasi-Physical)

This is The "blueprint" or structure for the physical incarnation.

5. Collective Unconscious (Quasi-Physical)

The shared, non-physical, yet-to-be-experienced mental structures.

6. Subconscious Mind (Quasi-Physical)

The storage of habitual patterns and beliefs that have been formed but are not currently in active focus.

7. Conscious Mind (Physical)

This is the current "you" focusing on the immediate physical reality.

8. Physical Brain (Physical)

This is the biological interface—the transceiver for the higher levels.

9. Physical Reality (Physical / lowest vibration )

The final output/materialization of the previous eight levels.



**beliefs --> feelings --> thoughts --> behavior --> reality**
