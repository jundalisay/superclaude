### Death, Personality, and Reincarnation

**Asker:** When our physical body dies, does the spirit separate from the physical portion?

**Bashar:** The idea is not so much that the body contains the soul. The body is a projection of the soul. When the body dies, the soul has simply changed the idea of itself to present itself in a different manner.

**Asker:** Is the Consciousness that was my mother reincarnated on this planet?

**Bashar:** For now, our perception is that this Consciousness is still functioning to some degree as a spirit guide for you. There may be clauses in your agreement, and it may be that you will join that being in the non-physical state before there is reincarnation. Mothers are like that.

### Symbology of a Moth & Music on the Entity's Planet

**Asker:** What do you look like?

The permanent members of our society are no more than 200 million.

The majority of our population is impermanent, "popping in and out" to experience our society for a short period.
