## The Concept of Unity and Individuality

We have talked little bit from time to time about the concept of unity and Oneness and so on and so forth as you say, and we have mentioned from time to time that unity and Oneness is truly more the product of strengthening your individuality rather than simply blending with the masses and becoming homogeneously indistinct.

Each and every one of you is unique for a reason because each and every being is a unique perspective, a unique experience of the infinite. Everyone is all the different ways that the infinite has of experiencing itself, and therefore the uniqueness is to be cherished, not lost in the creation of unity. As we have described in the analogy many times, if you look at your world as a gigantic puzzle picture and each and every one of you as a piece of the puzzle, you will see very quickly that in order to create the true whole picture, each and every piece with its own unique shape has a place to fit and must retain that shape in order to fill the whole it was designed to fill. In order to create the whole picture, if that piece attempts to change its shape and not be what it is, then it doesn't fit in the whole and the whole picture suffers as a result.

So retaining and amplifying your unique individuality, being the fullest person that you can be, is fundamentally the fulfillment of the purpose of your life and allows the best possible opportunity for the whole reality to become more cohesive, more integrated, to act in a more holistic manner. Saying that you are a piece of the whole is not in any way meant to diminish the idea that you contain, each and every one of you, the total sum of all that is, but expressed through a particular point of view, a particular facet of the multi-dimensional crystal.
