## The Unknown as the Mechanism of Manifestation

You should allow the unknown to become your best friend. Each and every one of you will begin to realize more and more individually at different rates that when we talk about the idea of going forward with intention to create the reality you want, yet at the same time go forward without expectation at all that that reality you envision must come into manifestation exactly as you see it. What you are doing is learning to trust the whole concept of the unknown.


Allowing the unknown to be a large portion of your life, to be the mechanism itself, the space itself, the dimension itself in which all that you wish to manifest can be manifested. Even though it may not always be exactly as you imagined to begin with, the unknown has always been there for you to help you bring about those things that you want more easily, more readily.

Yet your society for thousands of years has declared the unknown as an anathema, as something to be afraid of, as something to be avoided at all cost. To spend much time planning, making sure that exactly this and exactly that will go exactly this way and that way. To plan every single detail out so that you will know every single step in all of its aspects before you are even willing to take that step.


**A New Relationship with the Unknown**

Yet now in this day and age on your planet, the unknown is becoming more familiar to more of you as that quality that will allow you to actually arrive where you want to arrive more easily than all the planning, all the detailing that you have done in the past. As we have reminded you of the saying upon the planet that if you want to make God laugh, make a plan. For things never really go to expect. So why insisting that they should?


**Life Knows How to Work**

Life knows how to work. Life does work. You all are masters at allowing life to work. But now you're remembering that that's how it happens. That's how it comes about. That you let it come about. You don't have to make it come about. You let life grow. Let it occur in its natural way. Rather than thinking you have to force everything into position before anything can happen. Rather than thinking that this has to be set just this way, A B C D E F G, before I can ever arrive at what I want to arrive at.

---

**The Natural Order of Things**

Well, there is a natural order to things. You have heard that phrase in your language, "the natural order of things." Yet few of you ever take advantage of the natural order of things by allowing yourself to push in the direction of what you desire, of what you find joyful and exciting. That's all well and good, but that push is all that's necessary.

---

**The Sailor's Metaphor**

Just as you push a boat onto the ocean, you may have a general idea of the course you want to take. But as any of you know who are sailors, it is very rare that you can stick precisely to that course without varying a little bit here and there, without allowing for the unexpected gust of wind to come along.

---

**The Unknown as the Shortest Route**

Be prepared to accept the unknown as the shortest route, no matter how long it takes. Because in the unknown are all the currents that bring you really bring you what you need. According to the idea you have set out to be, they will guide you unerringly. Even if it may seem to be taking you off course, go with the flow of your life.

---

**The Unknown Contains All Possibilities**

Accept and embrace the unknown as that which contains all the things you are not capable of imagining. Yet it is your higher imagination. The unknown is your repository of all the things that your physical mind cannot imagine. Yet it is contained within you. You contain all of that. It's not outside you.

---

**The Unknown is Not Chaos**

That's why you have here the unknown because you think it is chaos. It is outside you. It represents being out of control, things beyond your control, when in fact everything that could possibly happen within the unknown is within you. It's within your dimension of the imagination. But the greater imagination, the larger you—that's all the unknown is. It is the larger imagination repository of all possible realities, all possible ways, all possible methods that could more easily fall into place according to the vibration you have set your intention to.

---

**Opening Up to Your Larger Self**

Open up to the unknown and you will simply be opening up to your larger resources of probability and imagination. It is not really unknown to you ultimately. It is merely temporarily hidden from you because of the nature of your physiological reality and the limitations imposed upon it. But it is not really unknown to you.

---

**Trusting the Flow**

Learn to trust its flow. Learn to trust its currents. Learn to trust the vibration of the unknown as simply a repository of surprise to delight you, to enlighten you, to entertain you, and allow you more insight into the grand scale upon which you really operate. You are never really out of control, never really out of touch.


**You Never Really Go Anywhere**

And remember, remember, remember—you don't really go anywhere anyway. You are always right here, always right now. And all these currents that may seem to take you to far away places and far away experiences and unexpected surprises—all of that's going on within you, not outside you. Within you only appears to be going on outside you because that's the game of physical life. It's just a reflective mirror. But it's all going on within you.

---

**The Unknown is Simply the Unknown You**

Those currents therefore are simply currents, ripples that are your own interaction with yourself, with other portions of yourself as you have created yourself to be in various portions. The unknown is simply the unknown you. It's still all you. It is simply that larger portion of the total you are forgetting about. That's all the unknown is. It's always going to be you.

---

**It's All You in Disguise**

No matter what you discover it to be, it's always going to be another variation of you in another form, in another disguise, in other clothing, in other words, in another style, in another culture, in another way of expressing yourself. It's all you doing a dance, a different dance. That's all it is.

So, let it be your friend. It will serve you very well. Don't be afraid of the unknown. Otherwise, you're just afraid of yourself, and that's all it is. Be delighted and surprised by how much you contain when you discover something you didn't know before. Be delighted and surprised by how much, how multi-dimensional, how universal, how infinite is the experience of your life. That would be our suggestion for how to make the unknown your best friend.
