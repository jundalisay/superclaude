## On Channeling and Past Lives

Many people who channel physically have, when they are in contact as you say with other consciousness, have for the most part made an agreement to do so before this physical life. 

That is not to say you cannot make that agreement if it is necessary for you during your physical life. But it is rarer in your terms. You will find that the understanding of connection will have been accomplished. The agreement will have been made when in your non-physical form so that your sense of timing would be very clear and understood. Do you follow me?

**Questioner:** I was contacted yesterday by Michelangelo who I worked with in the past life, helped a great deal.

**Bashar:** Only partially is that in a sense in your terms too.

You have within your own higher consciousness all of those abilities which when in your thoughts translated backwards into physicality are represented to you by that physical symbol. 

That individual does carry a great many physical individuals under his wing.

He allows his energy as a guide to touch many individuals upon your world. You have the ability to tap into that idea.

However, realize that in the process, it is for the purpose of allowing you not to rely on another consciousness, but to recognize those abilities within your own. Any entity in spirit form will never, ever, ever live your life, answer your questions for you. They will always be very willing to let you know that you have the exact same abilities and you may identify with them in that way. 

But they will always wish that you view yourself as an equal companion, as an equal creator. You will taste the flavor of their consciousness. You may recognize the flavor and atmosphere of their personality within their wishes for you. But always, always, always allow yourself to recognize when it is also your own abilities coming forth with those fountains of information.

When you are in the dream reality, you are experiencing all dreams. Therefore, you are in a sense perceptually far more aware and so in your terms far more awake.

**Questioner:** So, we mocked up this idea of being asleep for a particular reason that we're going through. Yes. So, here we are sleeping, wanting to be awake, and then we're awake wanting to sleep. Yeah. I don't understand that.
