## Primitive Civilizations & Space Travel

**Bashar:** There are civilizations which will seem physically primitive. Yet they can accomplish things that you have no idea how to attain for that is not your expression. There are civilizations that can do space travel yet have no technology.

**Questioner:** It's all basically the same consciousness. Then we are all part and all connected together with the same consciousness.

**Bashar:** Yes. But do not make that an invalidation of your own identity. You are equal to the idea in every way. You follow me?

**Questioner:** Suppose that I wouldn't want to be any part of any mass consciousness.

**Bashar:** Then you would not be here. Your reality is a product of your belief and your willingness to choose as you have chosen.

**Questioner:** Suppose I change my mind.

**Bashar:** Goodbye.

**Questioner:** Oh wow. Is it possible to change your mind? 

Yes.



## On Warring Civilizations

**Questioner:** With Star Wars and all this type of thing. Are there any warring people out there that would like to roll us up?

**Bashar:** To a degree, but they will not perceive you. 

Civilizations which reach space who still have an idea that they must create that conflict will not reach very far into space. They will be sliding around on the surface. 

The true penetration of space is the traveling within the self. And so to explore space, you will not allow that conflict to exist within you.

**Questioner:** Is there any reason we need to create spaceships on this planet?

**Bashar:** Yes, it is a product of your belief system at this time and will be one more creative way to explore another portion of yourself. 

Realize that you already have many other portions of your consciousness involved in other civilizations which have no need of doing so.

**Questioner:** When you talk about the warring civilizations and thought that they won't be able to, I mean it's like a sliding around. Is that sliding around simply because that vibration is so much different from ours that that's why it's like a slide and not a mesh?

**Bashar:** **Your journey through space is a journey through your brain.** 

The analyzation within space travel is similar as the exterior surface of your brain is used for analyzation.

You:
- will slide around only on the surface of your brain-space
- will not penetrate to that doorway within the brain which is true traveling in space.


## On the Dream of Invisibility

**Questioner:** I had a dream the other night in regards to my body, similar to as if it were a spaceship, but I was learning how to go invisible. And two people went invisible right before me and I said, "If you can do it, so can I." 

You're totally real to me where I considered real. And so I tried to take my hand and put it through a wall and it hurt. I banged it.

**Bashar:** Realize that being invisible does not mean you are teleporting. Understand? Your banging into that wall was for you a little bit of a sample of your eagerness. I'll say you're over-eagerness. 

Allow yourself to play around with the invisibility first. Realize that you had protected yourself in a sense. 

It was a symbol that if you had allowed yourself simply to be able to become invisible and then walk through the wall, you might have caused some damage to yourself. 

First become used to the idea of being invisible and explore all those portions of yourself through that viewpoint, but understand that that invisibility is also only a symbol, an analogy.


## On Remembering Dreams

**Questioner:** Usually when we dream, I find I wake up and I really forget about what I've dreamed. Is there a way to actually be in the dream and know you're dreaming while you're in the dream and really get the sensations of it and remember it afterwards?

**Bashar:**  Be in the dream. 

Know you are in the dream and remember the sensations after. 

But what you in a sense in your terms bring with you into this dream, this dream which is a dream, is what you need at that moment. 

When you spend time worrying about the fact that you forgot your dream, you are not in a sense allowing your energy to go towards that which you allowed yourself to bring back with you, and as such then you forget. But also understand that there is another side to that idea. 

When you allow yourself to understand that this also is a dream, then there is nothing to remember since you are constantly dreaming. 


## On Guides, Counterparts & Connected Consciousness

**Questioner:** Are there often beings who are linked together, let's say there's beings with bodies in other places who have an interest in what you're doing or tend to be connected to you mentally, spiritually?

**Bashar:** You mean in this physical life? Yes. Also portions of your own higher consciousness in physical bodies which are called counterparts, existing at the same time as you physically in one life, which are portions of yourself. 

Many of what you turn to be guides, which are I'll say in your terms some of which are outside consciousness, some of which are projections of your own consciousness but seemingly outside yourself so that you can relate to them in a way that is acceptable to you. 

Realize that in terms of what you say is reincarnational, you will always find that those you are in your terms closest to, you have always been closest to. And from time to time some of those individuals do not physically manifest with you but are still connected and sometimes may form your guides. Sometimes you may be their guide.

**Questioner:** Would that also be what L. Ron Hubbard has referred to as the math portion of an individual's reactive mind?

**Bashar:** Understand that there is some understanding of that idea, but once again a guide will always perform the function of allowing you to know that you are completely in control. Therefore, you will find that no allowance of your communication with any guide will ever lead you to think that there is any portion of your consciousness which cannot be known by you. Do you follow me?

**Questioner:** To some degree yeah, but not totally. Is there a portion of my consciousness that is not known by me?

**Bashar:** Only because you have told yourself that that is the experience you are having.

**Questioner:** If I wanted to change that and have total awareness of my total consciousness?

**Bashar:** Realize that as long as you remain in a sense physical, you will always choose some degree of limitation in order to experience the purpose that you have chosen to experience through that viewpoint. 

Therefore, you may be aware of more of your overall consciousness. But in terms of physicality, simply if you allow yourself to understand that you do have connections into all of your consciousness on some level, that will suffice to alleviate any of the negative effects that you assume must come from those portions that you have hidden from yourself. You follow me?

**Questioner:** Yes. So the fact that we are physical, that in itself as far as our total awareness of our consciousness is concerned, is limiting.

**Bashar:** Yes. But that is one of its purposes. If you can allow yourself to simply understand even with the limitation that you are connected to all your consciousness anyway, you will not need to experience physically that connection simply to benefit from the effect of knowing that the connection exists.

**Questioner:** Can we rely on the connection of the total consciousness to help us create any reality that we wish on the earth physically?


**Bashar:** Absolutely. In other words, it is called trust, faith in ourselves. Yes. Really it is knowing, not hoping, not wishing, knowing. 

Knowing that you are all that is and not needing to directly perceive it in order to perceive the effect that knowing has upon your reality, which then will tell you that you are truly connected to all that is.
