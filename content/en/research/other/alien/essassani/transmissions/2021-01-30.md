**H – Honesty**
The first H stands for Honesty. It is of critical importance in this time for you to express the idea of honesty in your lives. Of course, we are talking about the idea in general, not necessarily just telling lies. It is more about being honest with yourselves in your deep investigations of your belief systems. Do not ignore or gloss over; allow yourself to be truly honest about your motivations and what you believe to be true. This allows you to become much more transparent within yourself and have more ease in discovering the belief systems that may be out of alignment with who you truly prefer to be.

In this sense, honesty is about "know thyself." It is self-examination, not being afraid to dig deeply within your own mental ability to assess yourself. Be alright with discovering the fears that may exist within your belief systems. In other words, do not be afraid to be afraid. Know yourself more clearly and truly, and allow yourself to let go of those things that do not allow you to perpetuate that clarity.

We understand there can be circumstances where "little white lies" might be the kindest thing you can do to soften something for someone. However, it is also an opportunity to get in touch with *why* you feel the need to say something that isn't wholly true. By clarifying yourself, you may find yourself less often in situations where you are forced to say things that aren't completely honest just to impart kindness. Being more honest with yourself about why you feel the way you do gives you a chance to clarify that for yourself, making it unnecessary to resort to those little white lies.

**I – Integrity**
The second letter is I for Integrity. This amplifies the idea of being a holistic being, true to yourself. Have your own understanding of your ethical standards and your own concept of morals, and allow yourself to stick with them. Do not have contradictory or conflicting belief systems within yourself that allow you to act one way in one situation and a completely different way in another. Maintain the Integrity of what you believe to be true about yourself so that you move forward in a holistic way—not split, not in conflict, not presenting more than one identity or face to others.

This allows others to understand more clearly and transparently who you truly are, so they know who they are really in a relationship with. You are not fooling them or changing your mind every other second to confuse people as to what you stand for. Having the Integrity to be integral means allowing all aspects of your being to function harmoniously and synchronistically with themselves. You present yourself to the world as a particular frequency that is representative of your truth, avoiding confusion, misunderstanding, and misrepresentation.

**G – Gratitude**
Then we move to the G, which stands for Gratitude. So you have Honesty, Integrity, and Gratitude for your life, for who you are, and for your existence. This is the true core of what you call prayer: simply being in deep appreciation and deep gratitude for what is and who you are. This includes even the things that may manifest in your life as challenges or things you may not objectively prefer. Know that they are there manifesting for a reason that can serve you.

Be always in a state of appreciation and gratitude for what is in your life, not focusing on what isn’t. Acknowledge what is there, allow it to move forward after you have given it deep appreciation, because you know you can use it to enhance your life in a positive way. Therefore, it doesn't really matter what it is; be appreciative and grateful for everything that manifests, for where you’re at, what you’ve learned, and how you can move forward by taking those lessons with you. Don’t dwell on the past; be in the present so you can move forward into your future with honesty and integrity, remaining in a state of gratitude.

**H – Humility**
Finally, the final H stands for Humility. This is the idea of remaining grounded as you expand your Consciousness. Allow yourself to rely on the obvious and do not become too enamored with your expansion. Remain on a level where you are relatable, approachable, and inviting. Do not push anything on anyone, and do not necessarily need anyone to know anything in particular about whatever gift you have to give. There will always be an audience if you have a gift to give.

The idea is to remain grounded and humble with the things you are sharing. Stay centered and focused within yourself. Know that the importance is simply being the reflection and the mirror for others, allowing them to be of service to themselves and to become more of who they are through their interactions with you. That is the core of every relationship: to be there for the other, allowing them to become more of themselves, even as they ideally choose to do that for you. In remaining humble, you move forward as equals, with none being greater or more valid than any other.

With Honesty, Integrity, Gratitude, and Humility, you form a particular bubble state of being—a formula of vibrational frequencies and energies—that will carry you through in a sacred way. This allows you to slip smoothly through the eye of the needle and emerge as a being in synchronous harmony with itself and all life around it. You become known for who you truly are, with no hidden agenda, expressing your creativity and passion only to give the gifts you have to share and to be of service.
