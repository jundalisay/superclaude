Anything can be learned in a positive context from any kind of an experience at all, including the disastrous.

For they are only experiences as far as the soul is concerned, as far as the spirit is concerned. They are all capable of being added to the overall understanding, the overall growth of the spirit and the soul.

This does not in any way shape or form mean to diminish the experience as experienced by the physical personality. We understand all too well the idea that there is suffering on your planet. 

Nevertheless, many times individuals and groups will attract themselves to, or attract to themselves hardship and suffering to teach, to learn, to grow, to choose again.

From the soul perspective this adds to the ability to strengthen, to clarify, to delineate, to discern more clearly what it is you wish your world to be.

Three things can be gleaned from this earthquake. 

There are many more experiences and ideas than three. But this can start you off in understanding how it is we view these kinds of experiences.


## The First Context: Breaking Down Isolationism

Japan has been relatively isolated unto itself, relatively self-contained as a culture.

It has maintained a keeping to themselves, of creating certain kinds of sociological political barriers, lines, borders with respect to how they interact with the rest of the globe.

We are not in any way shape or form saying this is a negative thing. 

It is just part and parcel of the experience they have chosen as a culture to remain in a sense highly focused within their culture, highly unified within their culture in a kind of group mind focus with relatively little focus on the idea of individuality, although this has been changing in recent years.

Nevertheless, they have begun to break down these sociological structures, these barriers by realizing that they:
- need to rely on the globe for support
- can see the outpouring of support that they have received—that other people around your planet are willing to share with them, willing to give to them freely with love.

This will make those who have a mentality of isolationism and withdrawal to begin to see that:
- you are all connected to everyone else
- you are one family regardless of your differences, and in fact sometimes even because of them.


This is because any relationship is not measured by your similarities that make you compatible, **but whether or not you can allow your differences to be compatible as well.** 

Thus they are learning in this experience that they can reach out, that they do not have to fear the idea of interaction with other cultures, other societies, other ideas, other perspectives, other belief systems. 

Their culture can still maintain its uniqueness and its qualities without fear of dilution, while at the same time celebrating that uniqueness, reaching out simultaneously to the great diversity all around them—each its own expression of uniqueness, yet working hand in hand to support each other in times of need, in times of suffering.

So that they can begin to let go of the old view, the old world view, that they really are cut off from the rest of the world. 

They now can know they are part of a global family that loves and respects them and is willing to help in time of need.


### The Second Context: Nuclear Fear

They are the only culture with a soul path that has chosen to experience destruction by nuclear device. 

This has been within the context of their consciousness for many many decades on your planet. They have never really let it go.

Since the atomic bomb during your World War, they have had a love-hate relationship with nuclear energy. 

So in using nuclear energy they always as a companion walked with the fear of that energy.

Thus they have now given themselves an opportunity to see that fear played out, to see what such fears can do and cause the outer reflective destruction that represents the inner fear, the inner belief that they have not really come to terms with yet within themselves.

Japan and the rest of you on your planet, can now start a dialogue about whether or not that's the technology you really wish to continue to use.

Now you can see by example, by this culture being willing to act as the example even at what it cost them to act as the example, that this is not necessarily a direction in your best interests technologically.

There have been from time to time on your planet many individuals who've understood that there are other ways to tap into energy in the universe, to tap into the energy of your planet.

## BREATHING EXERCISE AND MEDITATION

Allow yourselves an opportunity to take a moment now to relax and close your eyes if you wish, and take a deep breath in. And as you take that deep breath in, allow yourselves to remember that you're all breathing the same air. Everywhere, all of you, there is only one atmosphere on your world. You all breathe the same atmosphere, no matter how far away from anything you may think you are. You are all connected in that way, and in many other ways.

So as you breathe in deeply now, allow yourselves to feel the connection to those in Japan, to those in all the other nations of your world, all that share the same air. Feel that connection as you breathe in and out, as you breathe together.

For is that not the meaning of the word in your language—to conspire—to breathe together? So conspire together to create the world of your dreams rather than the world of your nightmares. Breathe together as one, and the breath of all will support the breath of each, so each can be a manifestation of their dreams. And in each being allowed to be the unique individual that you are will support the totality of the whole picture, so that all may share in what each has to give.

Be of help to each other. Take action for each other, and you will allow, you will reflect, you will represent the ability to make manifest and make visible the world that you prefer—that is right here and right now but is invisible to your senses until such time as you are an example of the vibration of that reality.



We will perhaps discuss more aspects of this in time as things unfold, for there are chains of consequences that are not over yet. Many more things will unfold that have in that sense been triggered by this event around your planet, here and there. But we simply urge you and suggest to you that when you see these things or experience these kinds of events, that you do not at first—though you may recognize the suffering, though you may recognize the idea of things breaking down, breaking apart—that you do not at first assume to put negative meaning into these things. For this is simply the way you have decided to wake yourselves up, to shake yourselves awake.

It does not have to be that way. But in that you have for many generations resisted the idea of your natural selves, thus then you will find some way to get your attention, to bring your attention into focus, to bring your attention to the matters at hand that require your attention, that require your action, that require your vibration be higher, so that you can allow yourselves to live the reality you say you wish to live.

As these things unfold, no, you are not alone. You are never alone. You are never isolated, for all is one, even as it expresses itself through individuals. All is one. And if you allow yourselves to put in the positive meaning to all events, then the synchronicity that you will create will be capable of in a sense showing you, reflecting to you, demonstrating to you that all things can come together in an easy and effortless way, and that you do not require the idea of disasters to get your attention.

But as long as there is fear in your hearts, negative beliefs within your personality structure that you are afraid to look at, that you resist looking at, resist transforming, so too will the idea of events continue to get your attention, to shake yourselves awake, until such time as you finally realize what the message is you are attempting to deliver to yourselves.

So we suggest that you wake up now. Wake up and live the dream instead of just dreaming it and hoping that one day it might come to pass. Take action now and allow the dream to manifest. For it isn't that you make these things happen; it is that you allow the thing that already exists to become visible through your actions.

We thank you for allowing us at this time to point out to you that your world is growing, as you say in your language, smaller and smaller every day. And as in that sense it grows smaller, you become larger and larger and larger in frequency, and thus begin to approach the ability to have more contact—not only with us and other beings from other worlds, but more contact within yourselves, to all levels of your consciousness that you have created within yourselves, that you have compartmentalized within yourselves.

We will be addressing some degree of this compartmentalization of consciousness in the personality structure in what you call tomorrow night's transmission, and so continue this idea of how to get in touch with the barriers that you have created even within your own consciousness—that are represented by the barriers in the outer world—and how to dissolve them in such a manner as to function again as a unified whole, while sacrificing none of your individuality, in fact enhancing it to a greater degree. For the whole in that sense is the result of the validation of all the parts, the full validation of all the parts that automatically work together harmoniously when you allow them to.
