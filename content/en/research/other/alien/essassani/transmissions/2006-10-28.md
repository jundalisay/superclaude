**Darryl Anka (Channel):** This started for me, really, about 33 years ago when on two occasions in Los Angeles, I had an opportunity to have a very close, broad daylight UFO sighting—actually twice in one week. First time about 150 feet away, second time only about 70 feet away. An equilateral black triangular ship. That started me investigating a lot of things about UFOs and paranormal phenomenology.

Ten years after the sightings, I was introduced to another person who was channeling, and the entity coming through that person actually offered to teach a channeling class. I went into it not because I wanted to be a channel—I just wanted to learn more about that particular idea and see how something like that could be taught as part of my research.

In the class, under the guided meditations that were given to put you more in touch with other aspects of your consciousness, I received what to me appeared to be a telepathic contact. In that one second, an actual memory came back of having made an agreement prior to this life to do this. I understood that the UFO had been shown to me on purpose at that moment to get me to move in the direction I needed to move.

The question that came in the telepathic contact—along with who Bashar was and what I was to him—was: "Now that you understand you made this agreement, and now you understand it's time, is this still something you want to do?" It was offered still as a choice. It's not something that's ever forced on anyone.

I thought about it for a while because I wanted to make sure I wasn't going crazy or hallucinating, but I got enough outside validation to recognize that the most important thing about this is not whether or not Bashar is really real—obviously I can't prove it to anyone; it could be another aspect of my own consciousness. But the reason I keep doing it is because I realize that the information that comes through from this state, wherever it's coming from, has the ability to affect people's lives in positive and constructive ways.

Every time people use the information that he gives and keep coming back and saying, "I did this and it worked and it made these changes," that's reason enough for me to have kept doing the information.

Bashar has said that at some point in our near future, hopefully, he'll be able to sort of confirm and prove his own identity physically to us, but that time is not quite yet. That's what this seminar is all about—the idea of preparing for actual contact.

Regarding what happens to me in the session—I don't really go away. It's not like Bashar is here occupying my body. This is a telepathic link; I'm acting as a translation device, like a telephone. He's thinking his thoughts, and it's getting translated into the language I was raised to understand, which is English. We've actually seen him come through another person in Japan—a woman in Japanese—but it's distinctly his personality.

In the experience for me, it's kind of like falling into a daydream. I feel energy, I feel emotions, I see pictures sometimes that are abstract or literal based on what he might be talking about. I don't hear the words—that's completely secondary. When I come out of it, I'm left with a general concept of what he might have been talking about, but the words and everything just fade away.

It's very active for me. Sometimes I can actually have a completely separate conversation with him while he's talking to you about things that I need to know. It's a very interesting, very dynamic kind of process.

It's technically not a trance state. I've actually done this while being wired up to EEGs, and they've found out that while in a typical trance state, all your brain wave activity goes down. In this case, all the brain wave activity goes up—there's more connections being made than normal in the brain. Whatever the state actually is called, it's a little bit different. It's a very dynamic, active state. When I come out of it, I'm actually usually more energized than when I go in.

Some people have asked me—it looks like it's exhausting, it looks like it's painful sometimes—but it's not. It's just my bodily reaction to the way the energy needs to come through. Afterwards, it's usually very uplifting.

## Bashar's Opening Transmission

**Bashar:** First and foremost, we would like to take this opportunity to thank each and every one of you for co-creating the opportunity for this transmission this day of your time. Each and every time any member or group in your civilization allows us to communicate with you in this way, it forms a link, it forms a bridge between our respective realities. Through that link and through that bridge, it creates the vibration of a new third reality in which we can, every day, more and more interact more closely—if, of course, that's your wish.

### The Nature of Contact

The idea of contact with other civilizations is, from our perspective, not something that should be forced on anyone. One of the reasons why this kind of process takes a while is because we want to make sure it is always according to your choice, according to your preference.

There are considerations that have to do with energy, psychology, and the foundations and fundamentals of your society. All these things must be taken into account and gauged and paced so that the process of forming contact takes its cue always from the rate at which your civilization is comfortable in allowing such a thing to happen.

We will always respond to the invitation for contact from a civilization and have done so many times. But we always take our time to observe what the parameters of that civilization are on all levels possible, to ensure that such a process of acclimatization is something that will happen in a way that best benefits the civilization with whom we are making contact.

It would serve no purpose and no point at all for our presence to create disruption in your society. We understand from observing and interacting with many of you for many of your years that there are many individuals and many groups in your society that would find our presence disruptive according to what they believe a society is.

### The Channel as a Past Self

We will choose from the spirit level an incarnation in that society. The channel before you, the physical being before you, is my past self in your society, and I am what you would call in your future. By having a life, having an experience in the reality that is destined for contact, we thus then gather through that life, through that experience, an understanding of that civilization we otherwise would not have.

We would be far too alien to each other to even understand each other conceptually. By using our own past selves as biological transceivers, we are capable of opening up a dialogue with the civilization in question.

### Masters of Limitation

We have created a little bit of a nickname for you in your language—we call you the masters of limitation. But that is not meant in any negative way. From our point of view, in our experience, there are not many other civilizations we have encountered that have experienced the idea of limitation to the degree that your world has. It makes you very powerful, very strong.

You might euphemistically call your world a difficult school, where you have the opportunity as beings, as souls, as consciousness, as spirit to actually forget that you are connected to creation. For most civilizations, that's very difficult to do, but you have achieved it.

Only in such an experience of forgetfulness can you remember yourself, can you rediscover yourself from a very unique perspective. You are now arriving at a place and time in your cycle of life on your planet where you're beginning to remember who you really are.

### The Cycle of Forgetting

You have played out just about every kind of limitation on Earth that you can do—all of you in all the different kinds of incarnational experiences you have had. You have experienced being the victim and the perpetrator, the poor and the rich, the queen and the beggar. You have all experienced the extremes of the polarities that can be experienced on your planet.

Now you are arriving in a place in time where you are bringing all that to bear, pulling all that experience together, to remind yourself of how you can draw upon the richness of that experience to make these lives now truly more representative of the kind of world you would like to have.

### The Mirror Analogy

If you were looking into a mirror and you see the reflection on your face frowning, you don't go over to the mirror and try to stretch the reflection into a smile. You understand that would be pointless. The only way to see the reflection smile is for you to smile first—then the reflection has no choice but to reflect that back to you.

What you have come to believe, because of your experience with limitation, is that you have to change the outer reality first before you're willing to be happy in your inner reality. If you understand that your outer reality only takes its cue from you, then the choice to simply be who you prefer to be first is what will allow the outer reality reflection to reflect that back to you.

### The Meaning of Life

One of the greatest gifts that creation has given to all beings—and don't take this in a negative way—is that life is meaningless, absolutely meaningless. What we mean by that is that nothing has built-in meaning. Everything is fundamentally neutral. Every situation, every circumstance is just a neutral prop.

You have free will to assign meaning to life. You give life meaning. When you give life a certain meaning, that's the effect you will get from that circumstance. Whatever meaning you put into something is the effect you will extract from something—positive meaning in, positive effect out; negative meaning in, negative effect out.

### Unconditional Support

The universe completely supports you absolutely—always has, absolutely always will. There is never a moment you are not supported. If you believe you cannot be supported, the universe is supporting you in your lack of belief that you can be supported. That's the paradox.

If you exist, you're being supported. The question is not "Are you being supported?" The question is "What are you being supported in?" You will always be supported by the universe in your strongest belief because you have been given free will, and the universe takes its cue from you.

### Following Your Excitement

The vibration itself, the feeling itself, the sensation itself in your body that you call excitement, bliss, and passion—that vibration is your physical body's translation of the frequency of your true essential core natural self. Anything, any situation, any circumstance that contains that energy, that solicits that energy, that inspires that energy in you—it's a marker, it's a sign that that situation at that moment is most harmoniously aligned and most representative of your true natural core essential being.

The willingness to act on it completes the circuit, grounds the circuit, and allows for manifestation of all the things that are germane to that vibration in your reality.

### The Rubber Band Analogy

The degree to which you have chosen to experience limitation and darkness on your planet is actually a beautiful thing. The farther back you pull the rubber band into the darkness, when you finally decide to let go, that's how much farther and faster it will shoot on the other side because you're giving it all that momentum, all that energy.

When you do reach critical mass and cross the threshold, after straining for so long to hold it back in the darkness, it will shoot very quickly onto the other side and go that much further into the light. That's why your planet is so fascinating to so many beings—you're where it's all happening.

## Q&A Session - Part 2

### On Following Your Passion

**Question:** I find myself in a very mysterious field—music. I would love to hear any ideas about being in my position where I have ability to affect a lot of change and growth in people through my art form.

**Bashar:** You already are having all the impact you can ever possibly have just by existing. Creating your music will never create more impact in the world than you already have. What it will do is it will reveal to you and allow you to perceive—and others to perceive—the impact you're already having, just in a specific way.

### On Fear and Anxiety

**Question:** When I'm not feeling anxiety but when I'm vaguing out and losing consciousness, I want to have more presence of mind and more focus.

**Bashar:** Inertia is just the product of your belief systems of what you think is going to happen. Take it to the extreme in the negative direction and ask yourself: "If I did follow my joy to the fullest, what am I afraid would be the worst possible thing that might happen?"

### On Channeling

**Question:** I have the feeling that I'm supposed to be channeling.

**Bashar:** There's art, there's music, there's automatic writing. If you say something excites you, is there a way for you to tell me if it's something I've arranged before this lifetime? Does it make a difference whether you arranged it before this life or in this life in terms of its effect in your life? A difference that makes no difference is no difference.

The practice of channeling—many people when they begin to learn to channel, be it bringing through their own higher selves or connecting to another entity, it's representative of the completion of a circuit. When you're in a room by yourself learning to channel, it doesn't really complete the circuit in exactly the same way as when you actually allow someone to ask a question. That question opens a gate that allows you to connect and bring the information down.

### On Crystals

**Question:** I understand there to be a light body state of consciousness that Earth beings are able to achieve.

**Bashar:** The idea is to have a slightly different definition and point of view of what this whole concept of light body and spirit and consciousness and soul are all about. Your spirit is not in your body—your body is in your spirit. When you leave your body to go to other levels, you're actually just expanding beyond the idea of physical focus to include the body and more of yourself.

### On Teleportation

**Bashar:** Take a small object—a pen, a penny. Understand that object has a certain vibrational signature. That signature is what makes it what you perceive it to be. Now put it in your other hand. That's a different object—it's not the same object because it now has a different vibrational signature.

Continuity is an illusion that you create with consciousness. Location is one of the properties of the object. The equation of the object in one hand is not the same as the equation of the object in the other hand. The variable that is changed is the locational one—it's a different equation and thus a different object.

Teleportation is this idea: when you understand that location is a variable of the equation, if you are capable of changing that locational variable to represent the signature of another location, by definition the object must cease being in location A and must suddenly start being in location B because you've changed its equation—without traveling in between.

### On Death and Transition

**Question:** Regarding the idea of death on this planet...

**Bashar:** Death is actually the expansion into more of yourself. You become more of who you are in death. Physical reality in a sense is more like death than death is, because you place so many limitations on yourself.

Most individuals upon death will experience it as if they are waking up from a dream. It literally is the way you feel when you wake up from a dream and say, "Oh, this is my real reality," and then the dream just sort of fades off.

When you die, you still have a choice to keep on living until you make the choice to actually go all the way into non-physical reality. You are given the choice to simply go back and pick up where you left off. Many of you have died many times—you just don't remember.

### On Service to Others vs. Service to Self

**Question:** The Law of One material talks about moving from third density to fourth density and the harvest.

**Bashar:** Harvest is a euphemism. You are simply raising your vibration to be more harmonious with a new reality. The more you are willing to be what you believe yourself to be, you will simply find yourself where you need to be when you need to be there.

You are all infinite, eternal spirits. You are always together somewhere. Family in that sense is always family. Even if there seems to be the experience of temporary separation, you will always find each other again.

### On Sports and Games

**Bashar:** There are people that play games, but they spontaneously come together when they feel of a certain vibration to do so. They just show up and the game begins.
