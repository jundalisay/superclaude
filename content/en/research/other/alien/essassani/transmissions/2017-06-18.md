### Nature’s Web of Energy and Communication

There are a variety of ways that different elements, different expressions in nature, communicate with one another because you are all immersed in a web of energy and information. But there are very specific ways in which these connections in the energy web are expressed, depending upon the expression of nature.

First, we remind you that you do not exist in nature — you are nature. Your bodies are an expression of the nature of the real reality that you have chosen to experience. We know that for some time, many individuals within humanity have, in the past, thought of themselves as being separate from nature: maybe living in it, maybe being surrounded by it, but not necessarily considering themselves to be of it. But you are. Your world has given you the ability to crystallize a physical form out of the elements of nature that exist in your reality at your vibratory level of energy, rendering that energy, crystallizing that energy, into what you call the perception of matter.

You are absolutely an extension of nature on Earth. But in societies that exist on your planet which have forgotten this — not everyone, but many — there is an important need to allow yourselves to begin to experience yourselves once again, as you did long ago, as expressions of nature. This would be the first step in being capable of perceiving and connecting in a conscious way to the web of information, the web of energy that connects you all, that connects all nature, all things in your experience of reality.

We will begin by describing some of the ways different expressions in nature have of expressing this connectivity, and utilizing this connectivity in a variety of ways to support life, to support consciousness and its expressions in your reality.

#### Trees and the Mycelial Network

Trees are like antennae protruding upwards into the sky from the Earth.

The way they are structured, based on certain mathematical ratios, makes them sensitive to many different kinds of higher-level energies. They can, like an antenna, receive those energies in the very fine tips of their branches and leaves, and transform, transmit, step down, and allow that energy to be guided into the Earth. There, it can be used in a variety of ways, and tapped into by whatever needs to tap into that energy at whatever frequency is relevant for the different life form that may choose to utilize that connective energy web.

Deep underground, in the roots of trees and plants, there is another expression of this connective network — this social network. In any given group of trees, you will find in these forests that the roots of the trees, in whatever way they may choose to spread out, are also connected to what has been termed in your language the *mycelial network*. This is a type of growth of fungal filaments that attach to the roots and go from one tree to another, throughout the grove, throughout the forest. So in a forest where the trees are dense, all the trees are connected underground by their roots through this fungal mycelial network.

This network of filaments that connect all these roots serves many purposes. It is not only for transmitting energy or information; it provides a true demonstration of the intelligence inherent in all life forms on your planet, whether you think they are necessarily intelligent or not. Information and energy can be transmitted through the mycelial network in much the same way that your neural net and your brains transmit electrical impulses between all the different neurons. But this network also serves other functions:
- It can transmit water from one tree to another where water may be lacking. Trees receiving more water will know, through the information of the network, which trees do not have enough, and will intentionally send water to them.
- It will send food and nutrients through the network as well, to the trees that require it.

This is a true transport system underground for the trees of a forest. There is a vast amount of information and exchange going on under your feet when you walk through a forest — like an underground subway network that you may not even have been aware of, going on just a few feet beneath the surface. All this communication, food, water, and impulses moving back and forth makes it very busy down there; it is its own kind of city.

Sometimes you can tell where these networks might be, because one of the offshoots of the fungal filaments is that, at a certain point, mushrooms and other fungal forms will come up through the ground. They show you exactly where some of these mycelial networks have blossomed into another expression and form of life that then aids, assists, and adds to the overall communication web going on in that forest.

One of the things that has come down through stories and legends on your planet is the idea of what you call *fairy rings* — usually described as mushrooms growing in a circle. These rings are said to attract different forms of nature spirits and elementals, or to be places where you may find them gathering, if you are able to perceive them. This is because the ring is their form of connecting to the mycelial network. They actually use the ring, and the reason it grows in a ring, is to allow other beings of different frequencies to tap into that network when they need to.

Euphemistically, you could say that fairy rings are almost like their version of Stonehenge. The circular form captures energy and transmits it in a very efficient way, forming a kind of energy web lens. This helps aid and assist in the amplification of the energy and information, and also transmits it to the particular vibration needed for the elementals and nature spirits to be able to access that information, based on their nature.

### Minerals, Rocks, and the Deep Resonance Field

There are other forms of communication and social networks in nature. When you talk about the elements themselves — not elementals, but the hard minerals, rocks, boulders, hills, and mountains — there is another system that runs alongside what we have described, which uses the energy web in a different way.

One thing that only very few of your scientists have begun to realize and investigate is that there is what we might term a *deep resonance field*. This exists generally on a vibratory level that is below your usual ability to sense. If you are sensitive enough, you can feel it and tap into it, but generally speaking, it is not always audible to you. It is a very deep, thrumming, vibrating, resonant energy — part of the electromagnetic field of your planet — that forms a communication network between all the crystalline minerals, rocks, and mountains. This is the vibratory level of the consciousness of mineral or crystalline life, and it communicates readily in a variety of ways through that deep resonance field.

Some scientists are also beginning to understand and realize that everything is made of consciousness; everything is self-aware in its own way. It may not be self-aware in the way that you express it, but nevertheless, it is still a reflection. It knows itself as a reflection of all that is, in its own way and its own interpretation. They are recognizing that consciousness is pervasive and permeates everything in creation. A growing number of quantum physicists are beginning to explore this; the term they use is *panpsychism*, or the idea that there exists a *protoconsciousness field* everywhere, out of which consciousness in its various forms and expressions may arise.

#### Animals, Humans, and the Consciousness Field
The protoconsciousness field itself is another way that many beings throughout creation form this sort of energy net and web. This is generally what is used by what you would call animals and humans on your planet, in a variety of ways.

Beings tap into this consciousness field, and their experience is based on their own configuration of consciousness. In other words, the kind of being you are is a description of how you are energetically arranged as a kind of matrix. You filter this consciousness field through yourself and express it in ways that are genuine and relevant to what and who you are.

Animals and humans utilize the consciousness field and filter it through their own structure to create different frequencies and vibrations. These are given off and are specific to those particular types of beings. This is what it means when you learn to “talk cat,” “talk dog,” “talk fox,” “talk dolphin,” or communicate with any kind of animal. You are picking up on the specific resonant frequency of the consciousness field that they use — their particular wavelength, their specific radio station — and tuning yourself to those frequencies.

Now, humanity is not better than any other form of life, but it does have one interesting and unusual difference. Other animals can do this if they need to, but human beings, as sentient beings, have the built-in ability to actually go up and down the range of these frequencies we are describing. You can tune in and funnel through yourself all the different wavelengths, frequencies, and forms of communication and resonance. You can express an amalgamation, a combination, a kind of synergy of all these ideas.

You are the form on Earth that can process all of this into one large, overall understanding and expression of wholeness on your planet. This is why you think of yourselves in many ways as the stewards of the Earth — just as dolphins and whales are the stewards of the sea. They also have this ability to naturally move through a range of frequencies, to pick up, tie together, link, and express synergistically the idea of many different kinds of forms of life and expressions on your planet. This is why, in a sense, it is given to you to be the caretakers.

In the past, because of negative definitions and belief systems, many humans have thought that being a steward meant having domination over all other life. This is not the case. In some of your ancient scriptures, where it says mankind has “dominion” over the Earth and all life, the ancient term meant *caretaker*, not overlord. Dominion means you are given the role to process, synergize, caretake, and represent all the different forms of life, and to nurture, support, and give life to all of them. You do this in a way that creates a holistic web to be amplified and magnified, so that all life forms can pick up on the information they require to sustain themselves in the best, most beneficial way for all concerned.

You have the ability to actually become the frequency of the web itself. Thus, in any given situation or circumstance, you can connect into whatever kind of consciousness expression you need to — be it trees, animals, or rocks — simply by being sensitive enough to the fact that you are the one who synthesizes. You go within yourself to develop a more precise ability to tell the difference between the different frequencies expressed by different forms of life. By honing in on those frequencies, you can have ongoing communication and exchange of information with all forms of life. You form your own nature social network on a certain resonant frequency that is itself representative of the combination of all frequencies.

Everything expresses itself in a different way, through a different modality. It is about really tuning in and, in a sense, taking on the nature of that modality, and expressing it as strongly as possible through your own reflective aspects.

### Elementals and Nature Spirits
Nature spirits and elementals use the plasticity of the electromagnetic field to create a representational form that you can perceive, in order to communicate with you. These forms are not really physical; you call them fairies or elementals.

It is like having a sheet of rubber, where the sheet is the electromagnetic field. You take your hand and push it into the sheet; a person on the other side will see the imprint or outline of your hand. They are not seeing your hand itself, but the outline. That outline would be considered an elemental, even though the consciousness behind it is invisible. The elemental has to take on a form that you can perceive by impressing itself into the sheet, so that you have something to see.

They are forms of consciousness that exist within the electromagnetic expression of your world. They are aspects of nature that need a medium through which to express themselves in a way you can perceive; otherwise, you would not be able to experience them at all.

### Connection to Weather and Environment
Many people have the ability to be on a vibratory level with the weather and communicate with it. You can make an arrangement for the weather to create whatever experience you wish. This is becoming more and more common. It uses synchronicity and communication with the vibration of the idea of the elementals, making an agreement or arrangement to experience a certain condition, such as rain or sun.
