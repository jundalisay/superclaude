## Soulmates 

Soulmates are people who you need to interact with at that time. 


## Breakups and Closure

Breakups mean that people are changing and they need to grow in a another direction. 

Make sure you break up to move towards the positive, instead of running away from something.

You should let the other person take it in the way they want. 

Tell the other person in the most positive way that the change is good for both. 

He or she doesn't have to agree as long as you are honest that the breakup is for the best of both.
