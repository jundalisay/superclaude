## Elementals and Nature Spirits

Elementals and Nature Spirits differ only in presentation, of how they present themselves. 

Eveything has a spirit form. 

There is conscious energy field connected to the Earth, as the spirit form of the Earth. 

This field is very malleable. 

There are individual non-physical beings in this field (self-aware patterns of consciousness). 

They can manipulate this field to present themselves physically. This is why they are shape shifters and can come and go by switching between physical and energy fields. 

You cannot sense them if your senses do not extend that far into their non-physical domain.
