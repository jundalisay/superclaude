<sup>1</sup> In year 6, month 6, day 5 I sat in my house.

The elders of Judah sat before me. The hand of Enki fell there on me.


<sup>2</sup> Then I beheld, and lo a likeness as the appearance of fire: from the appearance of his loins even downward, fire.

From his loins even upward, as the appearance of brightness, as the colour of amber.

<sup>3</sup> He put forth the form of an hand, and took me by a lock of my head.

The spirit lifted me up between the earth and the heaven, and brought me in the visions of God to Jerusalem, to the door of the inner gate that looketh toward the north; where was the seat of the image of jealousy, which provoketh to jealousy.

<sup>4</sup> The glory of Enki of Israel was there, according to the vision that I saw in the plain.

<sup>5</sup> Then said he unto me:

{{< l a="Enki" >}}
Son of man, lift up thine eyes now the way toward the north.
{{< /l >}}

So I lifted up mine eyes the way toward the north, and behold northward at the gate of the altar this image of jealousy in the entry. 

{{< l a="Enki" >}}
<sup>6</sup> [To Ezekiel] Son of man, seest you what they do? [even] the great abominations that the Israelites committeth here, that I should go far off from my sanctuary? but turn you yet again, [and] you shalt see greater abominations.
{{< /l >}}

<sup>7</sup> He brought me to the door of the court. I saw a hole in the wall. 

<sup>8</sup> Then said he unto me:

{{< l a="Enki" >}}
Son of man, dig now in the wall: and when I had digged in the wall, behold a door.

<sup>9</sup> Go in, and behold the wicked abominations that they do here. 
{{< /l >}}


<sup>10</sup> So I went in and saw every form of:
- creeping things
- abominable beasts
- all the idols of the Israelites portrayed on the wall around 


<sup>11</sup> There stood before them 70 men of the ancients of the Israelites.

In the midst of them stood Jaazaniah the son of Shaphan, with every man his censer in his hand and a thick cloud of incense went up.

<sup>12</sup> Then said he unto me:

{{< l a="Enki" >}}
Son of man, have you seen what the ancients of the Israelites do in the dark, every man in the chambers of his imagery? They say, The LORD seeth us not; Enki has forsaken the earth.
{{< /l >}}
