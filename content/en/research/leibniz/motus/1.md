
**THEORIA MOTUS ABSTRACTI**
*(Theory of Abstract Motion)*
**Or, Universal Reasons for Motions, Independent of Sense and Phenomena.**

* **Author:** G. G. L. L. (Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz)
* **Publication City:** London
* **Publisher:** J. Martyn, Printer to the Royal Society
* **Year:** 1671 (M.DC. LXXI)
