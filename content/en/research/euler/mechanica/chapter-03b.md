## Definition 15

200. Hereafter we will call the height corresponding to the speed that height, from which a weight falling to the surface of the earth, acquires that same speed.


### Corollary 1

201. This height must therefore be as the square of the speed, to which it refers. With the speed c arising and with the due height v, v shall be as c2.
