## DEFINITION 6

48. The extent [or scale] of the speed is represented by a curve, the attached lines [y co-ordinates] of which represent the speeds, which the body has at places corresponding to the distances that the body has traversed. 

Thus of the body moving along the right line AM (Fig. 6) the scale of the motion is the curve AN, and of which the attached lines MN sets forth the speed of the body at some individual point


## DEFINITION 7

49. The scale of the times is a curve, of which the applied lines [y co-ordinates] represent the times at which the corresponding parts of the distance have been completed in the motion. 

Thus if the curve AT (Fig. 6) were of this kind, [p. 19] so that any attached line of this MT [y co-ordinate] presents the time in which the distance AM is traversed, then AT will be the curve with the scale of the times.
