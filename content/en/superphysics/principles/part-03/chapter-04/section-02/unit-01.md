## 1st Density 

This is pure identity, experienced as 1st and 2nd dimensions. This has linear self-time as the 2nd dimension.

## 2nd Density 

This is the self, experienced as 1st, 2nd, 3rd dimensions.

## 3rd Density 

This is the collective, experienced as 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th dimensions. This has spatial time as the 4th dimension.

This allows the formation of societies, organizations, collectives.
