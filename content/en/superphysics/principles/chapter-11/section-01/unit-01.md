We define "Spirit" as a mind in active form with waves of forces and feelings flowing through it.

This is the opposite of "Matter" which is a mind or node made up of crude waves and so is inactive.

Spirituality is therefore the opposite of Materiality.

Spirituality | Materiality
--- | ---
Abstract | Concrete
Dynamic | Static
"God" as alive | "God" as dead
Dominated by Positive Force | Dominated by Negative Force 


Since the Positive is the main mechanism behind spirituality, then we say it is essential to spirituality. 

However, when we solve a problem by working together (as the Positive Force), we do not regard the act as spiritual.

What is missing is the idea or the goal as the Supreme or Absolute Entity (the abstract God). 

Therefore, spirituality has 2 components:
1. The Positive Force
2. The Supreme or Absolute

The Positive Force facilitates the flow towards the Supreme which then flows into the Absolute. 

Therefore, we can say that spirituality is the flow towards (the abstract) God.
