Stellar vortices are of som1, with a mos1 center. 

These can be classified according to:

1.  Vortex rotation direction

- Clockwise: Stars
- Anticlockwise: Pulsars


2. Vortex energy quantity

- Large: Supergiants
- Small: Dwarfs


3. Vortex energy quality

- Strong: Blue
- Weak: Red
