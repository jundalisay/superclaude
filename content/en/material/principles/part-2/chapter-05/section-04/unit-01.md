## How a Planet Forms in Cartesian Physics and Material Superphysics

A liquid core area is What separates a planet from an asteroid or planet. This means that a planet can have a solid inner core, but it still floats or is surrounded by a liquid layer. 

A body that does not have a liquid core is regarded as a moon, asteroid, or comet.
