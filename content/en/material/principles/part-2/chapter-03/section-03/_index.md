The aetherspace constraints space (which is more physical) as to have an inverse relationship with time (which is more metaphysical).

Since timespan is superior to the aetherspace, then:
- timespan can be seen as the agent of the aether in the spatial layer
- the aetherspace can be seen as a crudified pure aether
- spacespan can be seen as crudified aetherspace
