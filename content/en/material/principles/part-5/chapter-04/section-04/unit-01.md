These elements are:
- found on the left side and in the center of the periodic table
- good conductors of heat and electricity, shiny, malleable (can be hammered into thin sheets), and ductile (can be drawn into wires). 

Most metals are solid at room temperature (with mercury being an exception). Examples include iron, copper, and gold.
