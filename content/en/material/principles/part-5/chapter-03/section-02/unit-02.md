 <!-- (relative to a perfect vacuum) -->

Absolute Pressure is the total pressure measured relative to a perfect vacuum (zero pressure). It is always a positive value and is commonly used in scientific and engineering applications where a constant, non-varying reference is necessary, such as monitoring vacuum pumps or in aerospace.
