Vortex Rotation is caused by the 2nd Rule of Motion. 

This leads to 3 possible spin actions:

1. Clockwise
2. None or mutually cancelling
3. Anticlockwise 


![Gears](/photos/stuff/gears.jpg)


This leads to forces:

1. Clockwise + Clockwise = repulsion force
2. AntiClockwise + Clockwise = attraction force
