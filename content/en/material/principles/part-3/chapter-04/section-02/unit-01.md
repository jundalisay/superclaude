The 7 Colors show the different relationalities in matter and space. 


## Why Are There 7 Colors?

There are 7 colors to match the 7 densities.

Density | Color
--- | ---
1 | Red
2 | Orange
3 | Yellow
4 | Green
5 | Blue
6 | Indigo
7 | Violet
8 | Colorless
