Blackbody radiation is the thermal radiation emitted by an opaque, non-reflective object.

This gives off light with color and brightness that is dependent on that temperature. 

- Temperature is the volume or density of heat particles in a body or environment. 
- Brightness is the volume of light particles
- Color is the spin speed of the light particles


Its spectrum is universal: the intensity and colors of light it gives off depend entirely on the object's temperature, not its material composition
