The spins of the convertible layer are opposite of the Radiant Layer. This acts as a heavier counterweight to Radiance in order to add diversity to the Material Layer under it. 


Name | Spin Direciton
--- | ---
Virtual Photon | Clockwise
Electron | Anti Clockwise
Neutrino | Clockwise
Proto-Aetherspace (Weak Force) | Anti Clockwise


This is why the weak force only interacts with left-handed or clockwise particles.
