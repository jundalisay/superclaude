## Superphysics Tetras from the Four Properties of Waves and Four Rules of Motion

In Superphysics, a tetra is a snapshot of a slice of spacetime with each slice being independent. A slice will have a spinning identity. 

Each spin as 4 properties matching the 4 rules of motion: 

1. Spin layer or element

This is the range that the identity is bound by the Negative Force

2. Spin speed

This is either the inherent or perceived spin of the identity. This shows more the Positive Force.

3. Spin Strength

This is how the identity is affected or not by other spins. This can show either the Positive of Negative. 

4. Spin polarity or orientation

This shows the positive or negative nature of the identity. 

This is different from math fractals which are just self-same shapes.
