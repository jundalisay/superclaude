Linear or sequential motion is an effect of the flow of cognition being constrained by the Negative Force.

Metaphysically, this leads to logic. 

For example, A leads to B and B leads to C. 

Non-linear or non-sequential motion is an effect of the flow of cognition being free from the constraints of the Negative Force. This freedom is given by the Positive Force (Yang or Shiva)

Metaphysically, this leads to intuition where you suddenly arrive at or teleport to an idea. 

For example, instead of A leading to B and B leading to C, you jump from A to C directly. 

We can say that:
- linear motion and logic is an effect of negativity and control
- nonlinear motion and  is an effect of negativity and control


Supermath uses both linear and nonlinear.

Qualimath uses nonlinear.
