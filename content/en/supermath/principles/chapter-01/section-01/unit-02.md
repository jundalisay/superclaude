While Math deals particles, and Supermath bridges particles and waves, Qualimath deals with waves. 

Waves are constantly changing and moving, but their general pattern persists and is different from other patterns.

For example, a pebble dropped in a pond will create waves that are very different from the waves created by a boulder.
