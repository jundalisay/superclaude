Supermath is the system of processing perceptions using the actions of the mind.

Historically, this filling was done by al-jibra which was used by Al-Khwarizmi to fairly distribute a deceased man's estate to his wives and children.

The voids were the unknown numerical values to be given to whom. 

Descartes called a void which is not resolved in physical reality as an imaginary number. 

So we classify numerical voids into 2:

1. A 'what'

A what needs to be resolved. This means it takes a material or physical form i.e. It gets parallelism into physical domain

2. A 'filler'

A filler does not need to be resolved. This means it keeps its abstract or aethereal or metaphysical form, i.e. It stays as an abstract effect that proves the aether. This is now called an imaginary number.
